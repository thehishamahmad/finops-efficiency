Overview

This assessment provides a business-friendly view of infrastructure efficiency and FinOps maturity. It helps identify where cost leakage may happen through low utilization, lack of governance, manual operations, always-on non-production workloads, inefficient storage practices, and hidden licensing dependencies.

Why This Matters

Identify Cost Leakage

Infrastructure waste often comes from oversized resources, always-on workloads, underused environments, unmanaged storage growth, and weak cost visibility.

Improve Governance

Budget controls, tagging, alerts, ownership, and centralized reporting are key to making infrastructure spending more predictable and accountable.

Prioritize Quick Wins

The assessment highlights practical optimization areas such as rightsizing, non-production scheduling, storage lifecycle management, and licensing review.

Support Executive Discussion

Results are written in business and advisory language, making them suitable for management discussion, discovery workshops, and cloud strategy planning.

Focus Areas

Cost Visibility & Governance

Measures how well infrastructure spending is tracked, reported, alerted, and governed across cloud and on-premise environments.

Infrastructure Rightsizing

Reviews utilization patterns and workload footprint to identify overprovisioning and efficiency improvement opportunities.

Operational Automation

Evaluates provisioning speed, deployment automation, non-production scheduling, and manual operational effort.

Licensing & Storage Efficiency

Identifies hidden TCO pressure from enterprise software licensing, backup retention, and unmanaged storage growth.
